What Rottweilers are used for

Rottweielrs were originally dog breeds to drive cattle. Later they were used to pull carts for btuchers. They were among the earliest police dogs and served in the military. They were also a popular family guardians.

Other uses

During the middle ages Rottweilers were used to hunt bears and guard houses.They were also used to keep cattle from straying at night. They were trained to be tough to also be able too control dangerous bulls.

Facts about them

  • Average life span : 8 - 10 years
  • Average weight: 75 - 100 pounds
  • Height: 22 to 27 inches
